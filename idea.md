## Structure

This blog will follow this structure:

- An about page
- A list page, where all the posts will appear
- A header bar with a Logo on the left,
  on the right links to github and linkedin and a About link, who
  redirect us to the about page
- at the right will be also a support for language selection: "portuguese" and "english"
- A Search bar where I could use for filtering the posts
- A theme light/dark theme switcher with the following options: "light", "dark" and "system"
- At the footer will have a copyright section.

This blog will use the Hugo static site generator tool
